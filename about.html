About Lifeline Connect

Bridging the digital divide through affordable connectivity

Our Mission & Vision

At Lifeline Connect, we believe that access to affordable internet and phone services is a fundamental right in today's digital world. Our mission is to help eligible households navigate the Lifeline program and connect with the services they need to thrive in the digital age.

We envision a future where every household has access to reliable, affordable connectivity, enabling them to participate fully in education, employment, healthcare, and community life.

What We Do

Program Guidance

We help eligible households understand and navigate the Lifeline program application process, ensuring they can access the benefits they qualify for.

Service Provider Matching

We connect households with the most suitable Lifeline service providers in their area, helping them find the best plans for their needs.

Support & Education

We provide ongoing support and educational resources to help households make the most of their Lifeline benefits and stay connected.
